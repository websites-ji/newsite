About BGTS

At BGTS, we bring together 25+ years of engineering expertise, strategic insight, and customer-focused solutions. With over 1,800 professionals worldwide, we help businesses thrive in a fast-evolving digital landscape.

Company Overview

BGTS is an international software and technology solutions company, powered by over 1,800 professionals. With more than 25 years of experience in digital transformation & engineering, technology consulting, and enterprise service management, we empower our clients to achieve success through a combination of engineering expertise and industry insight.

Our strategically located engineering centres and group companies in London, Sheffield, Den Haag, Düsseldorf, and other key locations provide flexibility, speed, and convenience, enabling us to deliver tailored, impactful solutions to clients around the world.

BGTS is committed to creating value for enterprises by offering high-quality, customer-centric, and results-driven solutions. The company has received multiple international awards and is recognised as one of the largest independent software companies in its region.
