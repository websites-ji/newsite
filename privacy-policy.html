Privacy Policy

Your personal data is strictly protected.

At BGTS, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how and why we process your personal data when you visit our website at www.bgts.com (hereinafter “the website”), engage with us during marketing and recruitment activities, or access our services. It also outlines your rights regarding your personal data and how you can contact us for further information.

Who We Are

BGTS (hereinafter “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a trusted partner in delivering technology solutions and IT services. Our website serves as a platform to communicate with casual visitors, potential clients, and job applicants, as well as to provide industry-related information.

By using this website, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not accept this policy, please discontinue use of the website. The website is not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect their data. For users under 18, parental or guardian consent is required for any data submission.

What Data We Collect

We collect personal data to provide you with a seamless and personalised experience on our website and during our services.

Data Categories:

  • Data You Provide: Name, email address, phone number, company, job title, postal address, or any information you share via forms or email.
  • Website Usage Data:Behaviour on our website, such as pages visited, browser type, screen resolution, and referral sources.
  • Marketing and Recruitment Data: Information from public sources or third parties related to business development or job applications.
  • Event Participation: Data shared for webinars, conferences, or other BGTS-hosted events.

We ensure compliance with applicable laws when collecting and processing this data, including obtaining your consent where necessary.
