Our QA Process

Discover how Terns ensures flawless software performance through a structured, end-to-end QA process. From evaluating test strategies to executing comprehensive test plans and managing defects, we deliver quality, reliability, and transparency every step of the way.

Our team of experts conducts a comprehensive analysis of your current test strategy, identifying areas for improvement and selecting the most suitable tech stack tailored to your specific business case.

In this stage, we engage in detailed discussions to define critical aspects such as quality goals, test levels, methods, acceptance criteria, test scope, risk assessment, coverage, and Pass/Fail criteria, ensuring a well-rounded and robust test strategy.

To align the testing process with your unique business requirements, our specialists meticulously choose the tools and test environments that best suit your needs, guaranteeing a seamless testing experience.

The Terns QA team ensures that your test workflow is meticulously planned and executed, developing new test designs that align with coverage criteria and test development, providing a solid foundation for thorough testing.

We assist you in executing various types of tests, including functional and non-functional testing, defect tracking, verification, regression testing, and providing comprehensive status reports, ensuring a meticulous examination of your software.

TTS QA experts rigorously evaluate acceptance criteria, offer support for user acceptance testing, and conduct alpha and beta testing, ensuring that your software meets the desired standards of quality and performance.

With TTS by your side, you can rely on our continuous monitoring and analysis of defects, ensuring a smooth operation of your system and prompt resolution of any issues that may arise.

We provide detailed reports on testing progress, results, and recommendations, ensuring transparent communication and effective collaboration throughout the testing process.
